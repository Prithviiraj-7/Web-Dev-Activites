Delicious Pasta Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a pot and cook the pasta according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and saut√© garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add cherry tomatoes and cook until softened.
  4. Drain pasta and add it to the pan with tomatoes and garlic.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and fresh basil leaves.
  6. Serve hot, topped with parmesan cheese if desired.
